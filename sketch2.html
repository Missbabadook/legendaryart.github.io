Colorful Dots

Colorful Dots, Processing, 2020

I made these auqa dots through Processing. I followed a tutorial on youtube called Circle Patter with an Array of Colors by a channel named "thedotisblack creative coding". This tutorial instructed me on how to create a circular formation of dots and how to add color and movement to make it flashy and included how to add mouse interactivity. This tutorial helped me create this colorful and moving circular pattern. Drag your mouse from left to right within the black space.
